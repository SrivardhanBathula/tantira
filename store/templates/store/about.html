Our Story

Threads of Tradition, Woven with Love

Who We Are

Tantira was born from a deep love for the timeless art of Indian weaving. We believe every saree tells a story — of the hands that wove it, the traditions it carries, and the woman who wears it with pride.

Founded in Hyderabad, we work directly with master weavers across India to bring you authentic, handcrafted sarees that celebrate the richness of our textile heritage. From the silk looms of Kanchipuram to the cotton weavers of Dhaka, every piece in our collection is a work of art.

We are more than a store — we are a bridge between generations of craft and the modern woman who values beauty, authenticity, and tradition.

✦

Authentic Craft

Every saree is handpicked from certified weavers. We guarantee authenticity and quality in every thread.

✦

Weaver First

We pay fair prices to our artisans and support their families. When you buy from us, you support a craftsman.

✦

Heritage & Love

Each collection is curated with love for Indian culture, ensuring you wear a piece of living history.

"A saree is not just fabric — it is an emotion."

We invite you to explore our collection and find the saree that speaks to your soul. Wear it with pride, wear it with joy, wear it as Tantira.
